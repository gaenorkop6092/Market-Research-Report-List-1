<p><p>The enzyme preparations market is categorized into six main types based on their catalytic functions. Oxidoreductases facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, while transferases transfer functional groups between molecules. Hydrolases break down compounds by hydrolysis, and isomerases rearrange molecular structures. Lyases cleave bonds without water, whereas ligases join two molecules together, often using ATP. Each enzyme type plays a crucial role in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food processing, and biotechnology, driving demand and innovation in enzyme applications.</p></p>

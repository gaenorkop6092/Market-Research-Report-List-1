<p><ul><li>Amorphous Graphite</li><li>Flake Graphite</li><li>Crystalline Vein Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end natural graphite market comprises three main types: amorphous graphite, flake graphite, and crystalline vein graphite. Amorphous graphite is characterized by its fine, micro-crystalline structure, offering excellent conductivity and lubricating properties. Flake graphite is known for its high purity and layered crystalline structure, making it ideal for batteries and lubricants. Crystalline vein graphite forms in veins and is the purest type, prized for its high thermal and electrical conductivity, often used in specialized applications like advanced electronics.</p></p>

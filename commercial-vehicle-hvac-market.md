<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle HVAC market can be categorized into two main types: Manual HVAC and Automatic HVAC systems. Manual HVAC allows drivers and passengers to adjust temperature settings and airflow manually, providing a basic level of comfort but requiring more user intervention. In contrast, Automatic HVAC systems utilize advanced sensors and controls to automatically regulate temperature and air distribution, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency. Automatic systems are increasingly preferred in modern vehicles for their convenience and improved climate control capabilities.</p></p>

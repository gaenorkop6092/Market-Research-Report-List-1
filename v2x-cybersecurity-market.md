<p><p>V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) cybersecurity encompasses various market types, primarily On-Board Units (OBUs) and Roadside Units (RSUs). RSUs, positioned along roadways, facilitate communication between vehicles and the infrastructure, ensuring safe traffic management and data exchange.

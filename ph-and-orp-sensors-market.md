<p><p>The PH and ORP sensors market features two primary types: insertion and immersion sensors. Insertion PH and ORP sensors are designed for quick, easy integration into existing pipelines and tanks, providing continuous monitoring with minimal disruption. In contrast, immersion PH and ORP sensors are fully submerged in liquid, offering accurate measurements over a wide range of applications. Both sensor types are crucial for industries like water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring optimal chemical balance and process control.</p></p>

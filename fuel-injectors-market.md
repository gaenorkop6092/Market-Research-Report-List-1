<p><strong>The Fuel Injectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Multiport Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel injectors market encompasses three main types: Throttle Body Injection (TBI), Multiport Injection (MPI), and Direct Injection (DI). TBI combines air and fuel at the throttle body, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for older engines. MPI injects fuel directly into each intake port, enhancing performance and fuel efficiency. DI delivers fuel directly into the combustion chamber, optimizing power and reducing emissions. These technologies cater to diverse automotive needs, from budget-friendly solutions to advanced, high-performance applications.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Automotive Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermistor Temperature Sensor</li><li>Thermocouple Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive temperature sensor market primarily features two key types: thermistor and thermocouple temperature sensors. Thermistor sensors utilize resistance changes with temperature variations, offering high sensitivity and precision, making them ideal for monitoring engine and cabin temperatures. In contrast, thermocouples consist of two dissimilar metals joined at a junction, generating a voltage related to temperature differences, suitable for high-temperature applications. Both types are crucial in enhancing vehicle performance, safety, and comfort by providing accurate temperature monitoring in various automotive systems.</p></p>

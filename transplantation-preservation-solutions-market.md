<p><p>Transplantation preservation solutions are critical for maintaining organ viability during storage and transportation. Static cold storage involves cooling organs in a preservation solution to slow metabolic processes. Hypothermic perfusion preservation actively circulates the preservation solution through the organ, enhancing nutrient delivery and waste removal. Other methods include dynamic preservation techniques or novel solutions designed for specific organ types, aiming to improve outcomes and extend preservation times. Each method serves to optimize the viability and functionality of transplantable organs.</p></p>

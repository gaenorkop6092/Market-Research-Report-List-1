<p><p>The mobile GIS market is categorized into two main types: On-Premise and On-Cloud. On-Premise mobile GIS solutions are hosted on local servers, providing organizations full control over data and security but requiring significant infrastructure and maintenance efforts. Conversely, On-Cloud mobile GIS solutions are hosted remotely, offering scalability, cost-effectiveness, and ease of access from various devices. This model allows for seamless collaboration and real-time data updates, catering to organizations seeking flexibility and efficiency in their geographic data management.</p></p>

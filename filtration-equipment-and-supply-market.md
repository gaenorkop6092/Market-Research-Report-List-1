<p><p>Filtration equipment and supply market includes various types of technologies designed to purify and separate substances in liquids and gases. Microfiltration utilizes membranes to remove particulates, bacteria, and some larger viruses. Ultrafiltration offers finer separation, targeting smaller particles and macromolecules. Nanofiltration is used for selective removal of divalent ions and small organic molecules. Reverse osmosis effectively removes dissolved salts and contaminants by applying pressure. Other filtration methods encompass diverse technologies for specialized applications, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>

<p><p>Melamine Moulding Compounds (MMC) are utilized in two primary manufacturing processes: injection molding and compression molding. Injection molding involves melting the MMC and injecting it into a mold, allowing for intricate designs and high-volume production. Compression molding, on the other hand, involves placing pre-measured MMC into a heated mold where pressure is applied, suitable for larger, simpler shapes. Both methods capitalize on MMC's durability and aesthetic qualities, catering to diverse applications in industries like automotive, consumer goods, and electrical components.</p></p>

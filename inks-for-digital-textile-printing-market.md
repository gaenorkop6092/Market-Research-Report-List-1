<p><p>The digital textile printing ink market comprises several types, each suited for different materials and applications. Reactive dye inks are ideal for cotton and cellulose fibers, forming strong bonds for vibrant colors. Acidic inks are perfect for silk and wool, utilizing acidity to achieve bright hues. Paint inks offer versatility on various fabrics with a matte finish. Lastly, dispersion and sublimation inks are essential for synthetic fabrics, transferring dye to fibers when heated, ensuring durability and vivid prints.</p></p>

<p><p>The aluminum cylinder head market can be divided into two primary types: gravity casting aluminum alloy and low pressure casting aluminum alloy. Gravity casting involves pouring molten aluminum into molds, relying on gravity for material flow, offering cost-effective production for complex shapes. In contrast, low pressure casting utilizes controlled gas pressure to drive the molten metal into molds, ensuring superior surface finish and structural integrity. Both methods cater to different production needs and applications in automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>

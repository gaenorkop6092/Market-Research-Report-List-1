<p><p>The Polyolefin Hot Melt Adhesives market is segmented into three main types: granular, blocky, and film. Granular adhesives are designed for easy dosing and application, ideal for automated processes. Blocky adhesives offer superior temperature resistance and are often used in industrial settings, providing high bond strength. Film adhesives, typically supplied as thin sheets, are perfect for applications requiring uniform coverage and flexibility, making them suitable for packaging and laminating. Each type caters to specific bonding needs in various industries.</p></p>
